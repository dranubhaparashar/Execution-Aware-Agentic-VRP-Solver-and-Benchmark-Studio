🤔 Why we test this

Depot reassignment changes the entire geometry of where a route starts. Tests whether solvers can re-anchor routes properly.

⚡ What happened

Depot reassignment forced 6 stops to migrate. CP-based solvers (OR-Tools, OR-Tools EA) re-anchored routes properly. Greedy preserved old assignments and ate the extra travel.

🎯 How OR-Tools EA won

OR-Tools EA produced the lowest distance (231.8 km) among zero-violation backends, with all 150 orders assigned and zero late minutes. The split-solve approach handled depot migration cleanly. Routes anchored at new depots, locked stops preserved.
