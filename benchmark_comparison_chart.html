Winner: OR-Tools Execution-Aware

The only backend that simultaneously achieves 150/150 assignment, zero late minutes, zero overtime, the lowest distance in every scenario group (232–240 km avg), and the fastest zero-violation runtime at 770 s total across all 18 scenarios.

Beats plain OR-Tools by 1–3 km per group and runs 98 s faster. The RT group shows the largest gain (-2.5 km avg) because locking in-progress routes eliminates re-routing noise, letting GLS search more deeply over the open portion. The split-solve architecture — locked stops copied directly from baseline, OR-Tools applied only to open orders — also guarantees zero disruption to routes already in execution.
